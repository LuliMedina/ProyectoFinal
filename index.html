who am i

I born in Ålesund on 5 September 1996. I have an older brother, Tellef (also a musician), and an older sister, Johanne. During my childhood, I was inspired by Adele, Joni Mitchell, and Neil Young. When I was 17, me and Johanne started a band called Sala Says Mhyp, which was named after our late cat, Sala.
